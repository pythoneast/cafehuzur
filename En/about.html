Cafe Huzur

The word "huzur" literally means "serenity", "peace", "tranquility" in Turkish. The name "Huzur" suits to our cafe. You can enjoy your meal in our cafe in a nice and relaxing environment. The distinguishing features of our cafe are delicious kitchen, pleasant atmosphere and excellent service. We are always glad to meet you!

History

Cafe Huzur was founded in 2007 in the centre of Bishkek. Citizens and guests of the capital liked our dishes, and we decided to open second cafe in Vefa centre in 2013. We plan to open more cafes not only in Bishkek but also in other cities of Kyrgyzstan. We aim to make Cafe Huzur the best place in the world!
